The fix correctly calls `update_model` when the model setting changes, but since the `FalAIKlingGenerator` instance is shared between the main GUI thread and background generation threads, concurrently updating its `base_url` or `model_display_name` during an active generation task can lead to unpredictable behavior or crashes.
